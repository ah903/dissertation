Welcome to Lockchain

This project is a demonstration proof of concept designed to evaluate how blockchain technology could be used to provide an identity and access management framwork for internet of things applications. This demonstration is based on a smart homes use case illustrating how remote locking and unlocking could be achieved and how these rights might be granted to different people or applications
